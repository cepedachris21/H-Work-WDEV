Frequent Questions

Check the frequently asked questions that our distinguished clients usually ask us:

How do you move between islands in Galapagos?

By boats or ferries.

What are the names of the inhabited islands in Galapagos?

There are 4 inhabited islands: Santa Cruz, San Cristóbal, Isabela, and Floreana.

Is there a boat or ferry that goes from San Cristóbal to Isabela Island?

Unfortunately NO, you must make a mandatory stop on Santa Cruz Island.

How much does it cost to travel between the inhabited islands?

The price is $35 each way. For children from 2 to 10 years of age, it is $30.

How long in advance should I buy the ferry tickets?

You must buy them at least 3 days in advance.

Can I travel between the inhabited islands by plane?

YES, there is an airport on San Cristóbal, Baltra, and Isabela Island.

What tours can I do in Galapagos?

In Galapagos you can do day trip departing from Santa Cruz, Cristóbal and Isabela islands, we recommend you visit the website www.daytripsgalapagos.com
